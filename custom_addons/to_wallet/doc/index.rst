Installation
============

1. Navigate to **Apps**.
2. Search with keyword *to_wallet*.
3. Press **Install**.

Instruction
===========

**Instruction video:** `eWallet <https://youtu.be/UXRZWelfB1Y>`_

Concepts
--------

When using this module, you need to learn some of the following concepts:

- E-wallet: An online account/service that allows one party to make online payments for bills of goods and services. To use the wallet, you will need to top up the wallet and conduct the above payment transactions.
- Is Wallet Operation: Informing the system which transactions need to be performed via an e-wallet.
- Wallet Amount: Value of money deposited into the e-wallet.
- Online payment transactions: Payment transactions using payment service providers such as Momo, VNpay, Paypal, Bank transfer, etc.

Usage
-----
**1. Configure wallet as a payment for a product**

On the product interface, select **Paid by Wallet** on the Accounting tab.

.. image:: 01-san-pham-thanh-toan-bang-vi-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: Product which is paid by e-wallet Viindoo
   :height: 480
   :width: 1100

**Note:** You need to install `to_account_accountant <https://viindoo.com/apps/app/17.0/to_account_accountant>`_ module to select this option.

**2. Deposit to the e-Wallet**

Navigate to **Accounting > Customers > Payments**, press **Create** to create `customer payment <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/finance/accounting-and-invoicing/account-receivables/customer-payments/how-to-create-customer-payment-in-viindoo-accounting.html>`_ also deposit to the e-Wallet. Remember to select the **Is Wallet Deposit/Withdraw** option and fill in the wallet amount.

.. image:: 02-nap-tien-vao-vi-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: Deposit the e-Wallet Viindoo
   :height: 470
   :width: 1100

Press **Confirm** to post the payment. On the customer profile, you can track the deposit amount on the *Wallets tab*.

.. image:: 03-vi-dien-tu-khach-hang-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: Customer's e-Wallet Viindoo
   :height: 400
   :width: 1100

**Note:** If you activate the `multi-currencies <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/finance/accounting-and-invoicing/multi-currencies/how-to-configure-a-multi-currencies-system.html>`_ feature, the system will record the wallet amount in the foreign currencies.

**3. Customer track the e-Wallet**

If customers are granted the `portal access <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/sales/sales/advanced-topics/granting-portal-access-to-customers.html>`_ to the system, they can track their e-Wallet by accessing to the *Wallets* section on the personal account.

.. image:: 11-Theo-doi-vi-dien-tu-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: Track the e-Wallet Viindoo
   :height: 290
   :width: 1100

Here they can also press **Deposit** to deposit more to their e-Wallet. Be aware that the deposit is only applied when you have done it before through the payment acquirer such as `Momo <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/finance/accounting-and-invoicing/account-receivables/customer-payments/how-to-make-a-payment-with-momo.html>`_, `VNpay <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/finance/accounting-and-invoicing/account-receivables/customer-payments/how-to-make-a-payment-with-vnpay.html>`_, `Paypal <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/finance/accounting-and-invoicing/account-receivables/customer-payments/how-to-make-a-payment-with-paypal.html>`_, Wire transfer, v.v.

**4. Create the sales order**

Navigate to **Sales > Orders > Quotations**, press **Create** to create a new request for quotation in the Sales app. 

.. image:: 04-tao-don-ban-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: Create Sales order in Viindoo
   :height: 560
   :width: 1100

When receiving the customer's confirmation, press **Confirm** to convert it to a sales order.

**5. Create customer invoice and payment by e-Wallet**

On the sales order interface, press **Create Invoice**.

.. image:: 05-tao-hoa-don-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: Create customer invoice in Viindoo Accounting app
   :height: 560
   :width: 1100

Select **Is Wallet Operation** on Invoice lines tab. Press **Confirm** to post this invoice.

.. image:: 06-vao-so-hoa-don.en.jpg
   :alt: Post the customer invoice Viindoo
   :height: 560
   :width: 1100

The system will inform that there are outstanding credits for this customer. Press **Add** to use the wallet amount for this invoice. 

.. image:: 07-su-dung-tien-trong-vi-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: Use the wallet amount
   :height: 580
   :width: 1100

In case the wallet amount isn't enough to pay for this invoice, press **Register payment** to record the remaining amount.

.. image:: 08-ghi-nhan-thanh-toan-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: Record payment for customer invoice
   :height: 420
   :width: 1100

.. image:: 09-xac-nhanh-thanh-toan-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: Confirm the payment Viindoo
   :height: 380
   :width: 1100

.. image:: 10-hoa-don-da-thanh-toan-Viindoo.en.jpg
   :alt: The paid customer invoice Viindoo
   :height: 410
   :width: 1100

**Note:** If the company deposits to the e-wallet, all contacts in this company can use this wallet to process the payment.

Now you can create `a bank statement <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/finance/accounting-and-invoicing/bank-cash/bank-reconciliation/manage-bank-statements.html>`_ and process the `bank reconciliation <https://viindoo.com/documentation/16.0/applications/finance/accounting-and-invoicing/bank-cash/bank-reconciliation/steps-in-the-bank-reconciliation-process.html>`_.
